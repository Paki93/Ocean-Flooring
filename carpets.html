Frequently Asked Questions

What types of carpets do you offer?

We offer a wide range of carpets including standard budget-friendly options, premium mid-range carpets, luxury wool carpets, and bespoke designer series with custom colors and patterns.

Can I get a custom size or color?

Yes! Many of our carpets can be customized in size, color, and pattern to perfectly fit your room and interior style. Our team will guide you through the options.

Do you offer installation services?

Absolutely. We provide professional carpet installation for all our products. Luxury and designer carpets include installation in the package price.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times depend on the carpet type: standard carpets usually arrive within 2–3 days, premium within 5–7 days, and luxury/designer carpets take 1–3 weeks depending on customization.

Are your carpets easy to maintain?

Yes. Standard and premium carpets are easy to clean with regular vacuuming, while luxury carpets may require special care, which we provide guidance for upon purchase.
